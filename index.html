(BONK'S SON)

In the early days of BONK, the internet created a legend — a meme that grew faster than anyone expected, powered entirely by community energy on Solana.

But what most people don't know is that BONK wasn't just a meme... it became a bloodline.

From that energy, a new identity began to form inside the community — something people started jokingly calling "BONK'S SON".

Not a leader.

Just the idea that BONK had evolved into something that could not be controlled.

And from that idea... BONK JR was born.

BONK JR represents the next phase of that same internet culture — a younger, faster expression of the original BONK spirit, carried forward by the community itself.
